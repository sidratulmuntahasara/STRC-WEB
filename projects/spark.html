Project details

Spark was a project under STRC for community purposes. It was entertainment-like project where you could get alot of knowledge, benefits and real-time prizes too. This project was launched from November 1 and ended at 31st January, 2021. It was a space where you could show your gaming ability and was a startup for you in the gaming world. There were alot of contests that were held. Not only the gamers but also those who were interested in arts, literatures, story writing,etc got Spark as a big opportunity. This project helped one to get innovative and did a good use of their creativeness in the field they were working on. Spark mainly focused on entertainment with education.

Ended on 31st Jan 2021
