In short, STRC (Scientific and Technological Research Centre) is a youth innovative centre working remotely on different aspects of science and technology with members from all around the globe. As being a youth-led center, we look for creative minds and work together to solve social problems. We are mostly based on bettering education, bringing technology to rural areas and making science and tech as a subject of ease and fun.

The STRC headquarters is located in Nepal and has its branches internationally. We at STRC, start from providing different scientific and technological faculty. STRC associates with its three Major Associates to perform in fields of Science, Computerized Learning and Environment issues. STRC has majority rights of ScienceX, TechX and DevelopX including all the other STRC program.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is STRC a Non-profit organization?

Yes, STRC is completely non-profit towards social development. Our projects are dependent on your donations which will in turn help us make a change to the world for a better cause.

Does STRC organize any public or social events?

Yes, STRC's aim is to spread awareness through social programs and to achieve the SDG Goals. So, STRC hosts webinars, hackathons, competitions, programs and much more.

I want to collaborate/partner with STRC, so, how do I do so?

STRC, apart from its personal projects, loves to accept partnerships and collaborations. To collaborate, fill in the contact form below and drop a message about your project details. We will then get back to you soon!

What happens to the money which I donate to STRC?

STRC, being a completely transparent organization, uses the donated money for educating students from rural and remote areas. All the donations are funded for the social-projects and none of the profits are taken by STRC.

Can I volunteer for STRC's projects?

Sure, STRC wholeheartedly accepts volunteers and always awards them for their great efforts. Just drop us a message in the Contact form below and join our Discord server to know more information.
